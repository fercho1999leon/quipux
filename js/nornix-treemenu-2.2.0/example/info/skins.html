Skins

By copying the files of a skin to the style folder you can change the look of Nornix TreeMenu. For now three skins are available in the downoad package.

  • Default skin by Kemie Guaida

  • Tango skin by Anders Nawroth from Tango Project icons

  • Classic skin by Ernst G Westlund

  • Library skin by Steve Greenham
